About

About

Is a smart comfortable café and restaurant, serving food continuously throughout the day.

Situated in the heart of Cuba Street,Wellington, New Zealand’s capital city. Floriditas is constantly striving for that magical combination of delicious tasty food, beautifully crafted wines and slick service. New Zealand produce influenced by the cuisines and techniques of the Mediterranean. Clean flavours that let the ingredients speak for themselves. Open all day we offer simple, delicious, tasty seasonal food to from local and national producers. Breakfast, lunch, dinner and everything in between. Sweet treats from the counter to tempt you and beautifully made Coffee Supreme coffee. Wines from all New Zealand’s regions and taste of what the rest of the world has to offer. All from early morning until late at night, seven days a week.
